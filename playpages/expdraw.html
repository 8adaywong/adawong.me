Experimental Drawing: A look into my work

Type: Art

Timeframe: Aug - Nov 2020

Overview

Experimental Drawing approaches the creation of digital and analogue image-making through collective and individual work and play. This includes brainstorming in individual and group exercises using different digital and traditional analogue (drawing, painting, and collage) processes. This class explores a very broad concept of what drawing might be.

Each week there will be a discussion related to a different topic related to drawing/imaging for analogue and/or digital production. A "drawing" assignment and a presentation of a movement in 20th or 21st century, a style or particular process or a particular artistsâ€™ work will be presented during class time each week
