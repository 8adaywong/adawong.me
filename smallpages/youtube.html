Why I started a Youtube Channel?

I have wanted to start a youtube channel for awhile now; however, over the summer, I really became interested in following through. I wasn't quite sure what content I wanted to post yet until I moved into college. I began with a move-in vlog, and from there, I started to do some other college and lifestyle videos.

I also thought it would be a great opportunity to share my experience at Duke University since there are not many college youtubers from Duke. Lastly, I just really enjoy editing videos, and it is great hobby to do during the small free time that I have at Duke!
